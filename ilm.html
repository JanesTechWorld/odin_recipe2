Irish Lo Mein

Ingredients

  • For the Sauce:
    • 2 tbsp soy sauce
    • 2 tbsp Irish whiskey
    • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
    • 1 tbsp honey
    • 1 tsp sesame oil
    • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • For the Lo Mein:
    • 8 oz lo mein noodles
    • 4 strips thick-cut bacon, chopped
    • 1 cup green cabbage, shredded
    • 1 cup carrots, julienned
    • 1/2 cup green onions, sliced
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 tbsp Irish butter
  • Optional Garnish:
    • Fresh parsley or 4-leaf clover
    • A splash of whiskey

Instructions

  1. Cook the noodles: Boil lo mein noodles, drain, and set aside.
  2. Make the sauce: Mix soy sauce, whiskey, hoisin, honey, sesame oil, and black pepper in a bowl.
  3. Cook the bacon: Fry chopped bacon until crispy. Remove and reserve the grease.
  4. Stir-fry the veggies: Add Irish butter to bacon grease and stir-fry cabbage, carrots, and garlic until tender. Add green onions and cook 1 more minute.
