Dafuqidat: The "What the F*** Is That?" Dish

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 cup cooked bacon, chopped
  • 1/2 cup diced green onions
  • 1 cup crushed potato chips
  • 4 eggs (fried or runny)
  • 1/2 cup gravy
  • 1 shot whiskey (optional, but not really)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the base: Heat mashed potatoes and mix with cheese, bacon, and green onions.
  2. Shape and fry: Form patties, fry in a skillet until crispy.
  3. Add the toppings: Top with crushed chips, gravy, and a fried egg.
  4. Garnish: Add green onions for flair and sip your whiskey for courage.

Tasting Notes

It’s crispy, creamy, salty, and just a little drunk—like all great decisions in life. Enjoy!
