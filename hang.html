Ingredients

  • Base:
    • 4 cups tater tots
    • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • Protein Boost:
    • 6 strips crispy bacon, crumbled
    • 1 cup sausage, chopped
    • 2 eggs (fried or scrambled)
  • Sauce of Redemption:
    • 1/2 cup nacho cheese sauce
    • 1/4 cup hot sauce
    • 2 tbsp ranch
  • Toppings:
    • Green onions
    • Crushed potato chips

Instructions

  1. Bake tater tots at 425°F and pile them onto a plate.
  2. Add bacon, sausage, and eggs on top of the tots.
  3. Drizzle nacho cheese, hot sauce, and ranch over the top.
  4. Sprinkle with green onions and crushed potato chips. Serve immediately.

Tasting Notes

Greasy, cheesy, and unapologetically delicious. Perfect for mornings when you need comfort, carbs, and chaos on a plate.
