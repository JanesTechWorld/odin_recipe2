Ingredients

  • Potato Base:
    • 4 giant russet potatoes
    • 2 cups Irish cheddar cheese, shredded
    • 1 cup sour cream
    • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Apocalypse Toppings:
    • 1 cup crispy bacon bits
    • 1 cup spicy kimchi
    • 2 cups BBQ pulled pork or brisket
    • 1 cup guacamole
    • 1/2 cup green onions
    • 1 cup Flamin’ Hot Cheetos, crushed
  • Drizzle of Doom:
    • 1/2 cup nacho cheese sauce
    • 1/4 cup ranch dressing
    • 1/4 cup sriracha
  • Optional Garnish:
    • Parsley
    • A shot of tequila

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Potatoes: Bake at 425°F for 1 hour. Slice open and scoop out the insides.
  2. Make the Filling: Mix potato insides with heavy cream, cheese, and sour cream. Restuff and bake for 10 more minutes.
  3. Apocalypse Time: Add pulled pork, kimchi, bacon, guacamole, and Cheetos. Drizzle with cheese, ranch, and sriracha.
  4. Finish with Flair: Garnish with green onions and parsley. Chase with tequila for survival points.

Tasting Notes

This dish is a declaration of chaos and flavor. Salty, spicy, creamy, crunchy—it’s everything you shouldn’t eat but can’t resist.
