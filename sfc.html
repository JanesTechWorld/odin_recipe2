    • Ingredients

    • For the Chicken Marinade:
      • 10 pieces of chicken
      • 1 cup Hennessy
      • 1 tbsp hot sauce
      • 1 tbsp lemon juice
      • 1 tsp garlic, minced
      • 1 tsp ginger, grated
      • 1 tsp onion powder
      • 1 tsp smoked paprika
      • Salt and pepper to taste
      • 1 tbsp soy sauce
      • Fresh parsley
    • For the Breading:
      • 2 cups all-purpose flour
      • 1 tsp baking powder
      • 1 tbsp garlic powder
      • 1 tbsp paprika
      • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
      • Salt and pepper to taste
      • 1 cup cornmeal
      • 1 tbsp brown sugar
    • For Frying:
      • Vegetable oil
    • For Garnish:
      • 4-leaf clover sprigs or parsley
      • Extra Hennessy drizzle

    Instructions

    1. Marinate the chicken: Mix all marinade ingredients, coat chicken, cover, and marinate for 2 hours (or overnight).
    2. Prepare the breading: Combine all breading ingredients in a bowl.
    3. Fry the chicken: Heat oil, coat chicken in breading, and fry until golden brown (8-10 minutes per batch).
    4. Garnish and serve: Drizzle with Hennessy, garnish with clovers, and serve hot.
