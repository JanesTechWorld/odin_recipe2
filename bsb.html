Ingredients

  • Dry Mix:
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 tbsp baking powder
    • 1/2 tsp baking soda
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Wet Ingredients:
    • 1/2 cup cold unsalted Irish butter, cubed
    • 2 tbsp cannabis butter, finely ground
    • 3/4 cup buttermilk
    • 2 tbsp heavy cream
  • Flare:
    • 1 tbsp chopped parsley
    • Gold sprinkles
    • Edible glitter (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F and line a baking sheet.
  2. Whisk dry ingredients in a bowl. Cut in Irish butter and cannabis butter until crumbly.
  3. Stir in buttermilk and parsley until dough forms. Shape into a 1-inch-thick rectangle and cut out biscuits.
  4. Place biscuits on a baking sheet, brush with heavy cream, and bake for 12–15 minutes.
  5. Brush with melted butter, then sprinkle with gold sprinkles or edible glitter. Serve warm.

Tasting Notes

Flaky, buttery, and magically green—these biscuits are a pot of gold for your taste buds. Eat responsibly!
